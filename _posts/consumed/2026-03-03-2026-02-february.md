## Doing physics with Ralph loop

[Teaching an AI to Do Physics by Wiping Its Memory](https://superposer.substack.com/p/teaching-an-ai-to-do-physics-by-wiping)

This was a physics professor who discovered the power of [Ralph Looping](https://claude.com/plugins/ralph-loop) an AI agent, slowly letting it build on it's failures and make new notes, until it does something most people assumed was well beyond possible.

> AI can now do research-level calculations—unreliably and with the right setup. That’s genuinely new. But verification remains hard. The gap between “got the right answer this time” and “reliably gets the right answer” is still enormous.

Reminded me of the [Brian Scalabine quote](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ftBTQMQ7myA&t=98s): At least at math and calculation, Claude is closer to Terrance Tao than you are to Claude.
